Recipe of the Day: Mantu

Description:

Mantu is a traditional Afghan delicacy loved for its mild and comforting flavors. It combines delicate dumplings filled with spiced minced meat, topped with savory sauces and yogurt. Often served at family gatherings and special occasions, it reflects the rich culinary art of Afghan kitchens. One bite of Mantu is enough to create a lasting, delicious memory!
